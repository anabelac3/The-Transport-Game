To recap. The companies transport 9 tons of cargo every day from Amsterdam to Paris.

Company A is a large company that transports 4 tons to Paris daily. Company B is a medium-sized company that transports 3 tons to Paris daily. Company C is a small company that transports 2 tons to Paris daily. There is an opportunity to earn a bonus of 9,000 euros. This bonus is paid out if the large truck is used. This means that any coalition of companies offering at least 5 tons of freight can claim the 9000 euro bonus.

So the negotiation is:

1) which partner(s) is each company looking for to work with?

2) how do the members of a coalition divide the 9000 euros?

Since the companies are competitors of each other, you must make sure that you manage to get the best possible deal for your own company. Which company you represent will be determined by a random draw. This is done the moment you and the other participants are matched
