Negotiation proceeds in three phases:

  • Phase 1. Make a coalition proposal: Everyone makes a proposal in which (a) they indicate which coalition they want to form and (b) they indicate how they want to distribute the {{ total_payoff }} euros between the coalition members.
  • Phase 2. Choose a coalition proposal: Everyone selects the coalition proposal they want to implement.
  • Phase 3. See if a coalition has been formed: Everyone sees which coalition proposals have been chosen. A coalition is finally formed when all the companies involved in the coalition accept the proposal. Each time this has not happened, a new round of negotiations begins.
